About the game

Warcraft III: The Frozen Throne is an expansion pack for the popular real-time strategy game

Key Features:

  • New Campaigns: You play as the Humans, Night Elves, and Undead, experiencing new storylines and character development. The main focus is on Arthas and his transformation into the Lich King.
  • New Heroes and Units: Each race receives new heroes and units, expanding tactical options and gameplay depth.
  • Shop Buildings: The ability to build shops introduces a new economic mechanic.
  • Neutral Heroes: You can hire neutral heroes from taverns, increasing the variety of playable characters.
  • Enhanced Campaign: The campaign is more engaging and captivating.
  • Improved Map Editor: Allows you to create your own maps and scenarios.

Plot:

  • The expansion continues the story after the events of Reign of Chaos.
  • Arthas becomes the Lich King and journeys to Northrend. The plot focuses on his struggle for power and events unfolding in Azeroth.

Gameplay:

  • It's a real-time strategy game where you build bases, gather resources, train units, and battle opponents.
  • The expansion adds new tactical and strategic possibilities.

Overall:

  • The Frozen Throne is a must-have expansion for Warcraft III fans, enhancing and deepening the gameplay, adding new features, and continuing the engaging story of Azeroth.
